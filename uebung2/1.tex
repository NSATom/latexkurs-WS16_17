Im folgenden die Maxwell-Gleichungen:
\begin{align}
  % use \mathbf because its going to be a pretty demonstration of Maxwell-Eqns
  &\nabla \times \mathbf{E} = - \frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t}
  \\
  &\nabla \times \mathbf{B} = \mathbf{j} + \frac{\partial \mathbf{E}}{\partial t}
  \\
  % \vphantom cmd for same vertical distance between lines. Takes into account
  % that fractions are taking up more vertical space and adjusts for it.
  &\nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \rho \vphantom{\frac{\partial \mathbf{E}}{\partial t}}
  \\
  &\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B} = 0 \vphantom{\frac{\partial \mathbf{E}}{\partial t}}
\end{align}
